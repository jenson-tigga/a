Georgeous Jenson Tigga

Jenson Tigga (born 3 August 2001) is an singer, arranger, composer, producer, and multi-instrumentalist based in Pune, Maharastra. Jenson's style fuses elements of jazz, a cappella, groove, folk, electronic music, classical music, gospel, soul and improvisation, and often features extreme use of reharmonisation.

Life

Jenson grew up in Ranchi, raised in a middel class family, with one elder brother. He attended W.John School in Ranchi, and St.Francis School for Higher Secondary Education in Ranchi. Jenson briefly studied jazz piano at the Swarnaboomi Academy of Music in Chennai.
